Create Efficient Merchant Experiences for Small Businesses

OVERVIEW

At NAB, I worked on designing merchant solutions for small businesses across Australia and New Zealand, focusing on improving both customer-facing payment experiences and internal banking systems.

My role involved enhancing the digital merchant experience across NAB’s payment and commerce ecosystem, including EFTPOS, online payments, and merchant onboarding journeys. I collaborated closely with product managers, engineers, and business stakeholders to simplify complex workflows, improve usability, and create more seamless experiences for business customers.

Alongside customer-facing improvements, I also contributed to optimizing internal banker tools and operational systems to help support teams manage merchant services more efficiently. This included identifying friction points in existing workflows, streamlining service processes, and designing scalable experiences that balanced business goals with customer needs.

Due to the nature of the work and company confidentiality, I’m limited in what I can publicly share. However, beyond product delivery, I contributed to several initiatives focused on accessibility, AI enablement, and design education within the organization. If you'd like to learn more, please feel free to contact me on Linkedin or Email.

Accessibility Workshop for Engineering Teams

Hosted an Accessibility from a Designer’s Perspective workshop as part of a broader training program for the engineering community, demonstrating how accessibility shapes everyday design decisions and highlighting the thinking, considerations, and processes that happen behind the scenes before designs reach development.

AI Enablement & Cross-Functional Collaboration

Alongside product design, I contributed to AI enablement initiatives across Business Transaction Banking, supporting the responsible adoption of AI to improve operational efficiency and customer experiences. This included helping shape AI-first frameworks, identifying high-value opportunities, and collaborating across product, technology, risk, legal, and governance teams to ensure AI solutions were practical, scalable, and safe. I also supported business analysts in utilizing AI tools to communicate more effectively with stakeholders and designers during early engagement and discovery phases, while helping teams understand where AI can accelerate workflows and where design expertise remains essential in creating thoughtful, human-centered products.

Design Starcamp — Design Thinking for Future Engineers

Supported NAB’s Design Starcamp program with more than 70 software engineering students from top-tier universities, facilitating a two-day workshop focused on design thinking and how to apply it within real-world product development. The sessions encouraged creativity, collaboration, learning, and community building across emerging technology and design talent.
